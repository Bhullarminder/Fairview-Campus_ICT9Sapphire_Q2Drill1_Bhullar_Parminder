EMERGENCY GO BAG ESSENTIALS



Medical aids


  • Emergency aids like the following are helpful when someone is injured or is sick.
  1. Anti septic wipes
  2. Clean Cloths
  3. Pain relievers
  4. Band aids
  5. Tweezers

Hand held tools


  • The following tools can help with locating and communication.
  1. flashlights
  2. whistles
  3. radios

Clothes


Clean dry clothes can help prevent infections and disseases like fever.

Emergency foods and drinks


Nourishing foods like dehydrated foods don't spoil easilu and drinks like water can help prevent dehydration.
